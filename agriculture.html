PLANTS

AMPALAYA

Ampalaya, also known as bitter melon, is a tropical vine commonly found in Asia, known for its distinctively bitter taste. It's highly valued for its nutritional benefits and medicinal properties, often used in various dishes and traditional remedies.

With the support of the Prelli Foundation, we're planting the seeds of change and empowering communities through sustainable ampalaya cultivation. Together, we can cultivate a healthier, greener future!
